Tips

Starting from your HRIS export (UKG, Namely, Frontline, etc.)

Export your staffing roster from your HRIS. It will have Name, School, Position, Hire Date, Status — the basics. Import that first to get started, then enrich with the school-specific fields (Grade Level, Department, Certifications) that your HRIS doesn't carry.

Open positions

Include open/unfilled positions with "OPEN POSITION" as the name and "Open" as the status. This gives TalentPoint the full staffing picture, not just who's here today.

Support staff and operations

Include everyone — custodians, cafeteria, office staff, security. Use "All" for grade level and their functional area for department (Operations, Cafeteria, Facilities, Admin). TalentPoint tracks the whole building, not just teachers.

Multiple schools

Put all schools in one file. The School column tells TalentPoint how to organize them. School names must match exactly — "Langston Hughes" and "Langston Hughes Academy" will be treated as two different schools.

Data stays on your machine. The CSV import runs entirely in your browser. No data is uploaded to any server. TalentPoint processes the file locally.

What comes next

After import, TalentPoint will show you what's missing and what to add next. The staffing picture gets richer over time as you:

  • Log touchpoints and observations (Stream 6)
  • Connect survey data from Attuned, TNTP, or UpBeat (Stream 3)
  • Track intent-to-return conversations (Stream 5)
  • Build next year's staffing plan (Stream 4)
